Simply Guacamole

Description

Mashed avocado mixed with chopped tomatoes and onion.

Ingredients

  • 5 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • ¾ cup minced green onion
  • ½ cup minced fresh cilantro
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Stir together the avocado and lemon juice in a serving bowl;
  2. Add the green onion and cilantro;
  3. Season with salt and pepper;
  4. Mix Well
  5. Serve immediately or store covered in refrigerator with avocado pits in the bowl to keep from browning!
